1. What do you recall from the lecture? An intelligent agent in AI settings is __________ .
Option - Autonomous, Proactive & Goal Directed

2. What do you recall about self aware agents discussed in the lecture? A self aware agent __________ .
Option - models itself in the model of the world

3. Models developed using artificial neural networks are __________ .
Option - a representation of past experience

4. Planning systems are used to __________ .
Option - compute a sequence of moves for the future

5. The Turing Test was devised to __________ .
Option - evaluate a machine's ability to exhibit intelligent behaviour indistinguishable from that of a human

6. Which of the following AI agents first demonstrated that machines can beat the very best humans at chess?
Option - Deep Blue

7. Tyson told Buster that he won. The pronoun “he” refers to __________ .
Option - Cannot say

8. The judge told Buster that he won. The pronoun “he” refers to __________ .
Option - Buster

9. The dog chased the cat, which ran up a tree. It waited at the top. Who does “It” refer to?
Option - The cat

10. The dog chased the cat, which ran up a tree. It waited at the bottom. Who does “It” refer to?
Option - The dog

11. The above questions involve anaphora resolution. For a computer program to answer the above questions correctly it would __________ .
Option - need to understand the semantics of pronouns in natural language

12. "I think that tastes, odors, colors, and so on are no more than mere names so far as the object in which we locate them are concerned, and that they reside in consciousness. Hence if the living creature were removed, all these qualities would be wiped away and annihilated". Who said it?
Option - Gottfried Wilhelm von Leibniz

13. Who first said that reasoning is manipulation of symbols, reasoning is computation?
Option - John McCarthy

14. Who said the following? - “Thoughts themselves are symbolic representations”
Option - Gottfried Wilhelm von Leibniz

15. Who invented the Stepped Drum in 1673, which was used until the mid-1970s?
Option - Gottfried Wilhelm von Leibniz

16. Who suggested that the Analytical Engine might act upon other things besides number, say, compose music?
Option - Augusta Ada Byron

17. Who said that the question whether machines can think is itself “too meaningless”?
Option - Alan Turing

18. The “Logic Theorist” is ____________________
Option - a computer program to find proofs

19. What is true of the first checkers program written in 1952 by Arthur Samuel?
Option - It improved as it played more and more games

20. Select the correct options. Simon and Newell __________ .
